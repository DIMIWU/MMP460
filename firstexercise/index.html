GENTLE MONSTER X alexanderwang

2019 FW collection

‘M.PRI$$’ is the second collaboration of Gentle Monster and Alexander Wang after ‘CEO’, initially showcased in Alexander Wang's 2019 FW New York Fashion Week Show. These new sunglasses are based on Gentle Monster’s iconic model with a smooth curved semi-cat eye front and bold temples, adorned with a fluid metal snake detail around the lenses front. ‘M.PRI$$’ will be available from 12th September 2019 at GENTLEMONSTER.COM and selected Gentle Monster flagship stores.
